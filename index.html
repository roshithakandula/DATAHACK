About the Hackathon

Rules & Guidelines

  • Team size: 1 to 4 members.
  • Submissions via Unstop or Devpost only.
  • No multiple team participation.
  • Projects must be original & innovative.
  • Internship opportunities based on performance.

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation – 15%
  • Creativity – 35%
  • Functionality – 30%
  • Business Model – 10%
  • Presentation – 10%

Exciting Prizes

  • Internship Opportunities
  • Cash Rewards, Mementos & more
  • Top 3 Winners awarded

Organizing Clubs

SODS Club

The School of Data Science promotes AI, ML, and data science engagement.

SODS actively works on applied data projects and hosts workshops on advanced analytics, Python, and data visualization tools like Power BI & Tableau.

Garuda Club

Leading electronics & robotics club focusing on hardware innovation and IoT.

Garuda regularly participates in national robotics contests, builds drones, bots, and offers hands-on IoT device development guidance to students.

Cyber Security Club

Focuses on ethical hacking, threat analysis, and secure coding practices.

They conduct Capture-the-Flag events, vulnerability assessments, and host sessions with security experts to prepare students for real-world cyber challenges.
